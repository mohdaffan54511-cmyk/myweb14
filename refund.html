Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to services purchased from Data Specialist.

Cancellation

A project can be cancelled before work has started. Once work has started, cancellation may not be available depending on the work completed.

Refunds

Refunds may be considered only if the service has not been started or if we are unable to deliver the agreed service.

No Refund After Delivery

Once the final file, document, dashboard, or completed work has been delivered, refunds are generally not provided.

Revisions

If there is a small issue in the delivered work, we will try to fix it through revisions based on the original project requirements.

Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, please contact us through the contact form on our website.
